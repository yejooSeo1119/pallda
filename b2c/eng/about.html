We are ‘PALLDA’

Our company is a bridge between Korean and Middle Eastern businesses, providing essential services to help Korean companies easily enter the Middle Eastern market and vice versa. We offer language translation services and documentation support to Korean companies looking to export to the Middle East, and we assist Middle Eastern companies in importing products from Korea.

Our team is knowledgeable about the cultural differences and business challenges that can arise when conducting international trade, and we are dedicated to helping our clients overcome these obstacles. We believe that our services are crucial for facilitating trade and fostering stronger relationships between Korea and the Middle East.

Whether you are a Korean company looking to expand into the Middle East or a Middle Eastern company seeking to import products from Korea, our team is here to help. With our support, you can confidently navigate the complexities of international trade and take your business to the next level. Contact us today to learn more about how we can assist you in achieving your goals.

What we support

1. Our company offers a comprehensive range of services to support businesses in Korea and the Middle East, including information verification, inter-business support, and documentation support.

2. Our information verification service helps ensure that the companies and products you are dealing with are legitimate and reliable, giving you peace of mind when conducting international trade.

3. Our inter-business support services include language translation and interpretation, as well as cultural and business guidance to help you navigate the unique challenges of the Korean and Middle Eastern markets.

4. Our documentation support service streamlines the import and export process, helping you manage paperwork and other requirements with ease.

With our expert support, you can confidently conduct international trade and grow your business in Korea and the Middle East.

How to use our service?

If you are a buyer looking to purchase goods from Korea, you can sign up for membership on our website and search for the items you are interested in in the "Seller" column. If you don't find what you are looking for, you can request to upload the product information in the "Upload" column. Our team will review and verify the information, and then post it in the "Buyer" column for sellers to see.

If you are a seller looking to export Korean goods to the Middle East, you can sign up for membership on our website and search for potential buyers in the "Buyer" column. If you don't find a suitable buyer, you can request to upload the product information in the "Upload" column. Our team will review and verify the information, and then post it in the "Seller" column for buyers to see.

Once you have found a product or project that you want to purchase or sell on the "Seller" or "Buyer" page, you can click on the detailed view and leave a comment expressing your interest. Our team will then connect you with the relevant company and provide support with documentation and translation to help you complete the transaction.

Once the transaction is 100% complete, both companies will pay a prescribed service fee to our company, and the services provided will be considered used.

Our team is dedicated to helping businesses in Korea and the Middle East succeed in the global marketplace, and we are committed to providing personalized support and guidance throughout the process.
